Minimalistic Wall Art

Welcome to Pinacle Moon Crafts! We create dream catchers, hanging wall macrame, and wrapped stones.
Our designs are minimalistic and use earth inspired colors. Our decorative crafts fit into any home design asthetic.
They also make unique gifts, so gift one to yourself or someone special to enjoy for years to come!

Dreamcatchers

Dream catchers can be crafted to hang flat against a wall or hung from the ceilng.

Macrame

Macrame can be crafted from small designs for a keychain or bigger for a statement piece to hang on the wall.

Wrapped Stones

Wrapped stones can also be crafted on a smaller or larger scale. The stones are rough cut, so they have that natural look and feel.
